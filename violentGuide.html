Violent Sun Explained


	Your third stop.
				Featuring Pain
	
  First : the pre-requisites and info needed before playing.
	-At least level 72
	-Sets don't matter cause youre not gonna have enough damage anyway
	-At least 2200 ACCURACY
	-Split up into 3 Phases
		=P1 is unlimited time , if you cant pass LF dont bother others
		
		=P2 needs P1 done in under 3mins
		
		  That means 70K AP(max atk + cdmg)
		  70 CRIT RATE + PERFUME
		  50+ PENETRATION
		  44 CDR
		  (All of this to beat P1 in under 3 mins)
		  
		 =P3 Needs P2 done in under 3 mins
		 During P2 his armor is PRETTY HIGH NOW
		   That menas 80k+ AP
		   70 CRIT RATE + PERFUME
		   80+ PENETRATION
		   44 CDR
		   IO / ROSKA /CHLOE AR card
		    
		(After clearing P2 you dont have a timer anymore , so stats don't matter after that)
		     
   Second : The gear and order you should get it
   
	-Weapon : ST(a bit bad) EX HR
	  
		=HR is guaranteed after 300 runs
		=Unless you can do P3 you should get EX weapon +9
		
	-Armor : You can use any ST EX HR and still get 5-set bonus !! use this
	   
	   
	   
		=4/5 is good , 5/5 is even better
		
	-Accesories : Not a big rush , the auto-revive is pretty cool 
	   
		=can be the first to swap if you aim for P3
		
   Third : Mechanics
	-Stomp 
		=2KO hits , 1 delay , another KO 
		 stay in air to avoid , red circles do dmg even in air
	-Circle 1 2 3
		= one inner circle , one outer circle , JUMP to avoid last circle
	-Grab
		=Omae wa mou shindeiru ... NANI? (dodge , hes invulnerable during spin)
	-3 strike circle
		=Hits a bit hard , and spawns 3 circles that explode with delay , just avoid them
	-Highway Rush
		=This is the move that decides whether you pass or fail P3
		  He will stun everyone , jump in the middle and choose one person to dash into
		  You have plenty of time to AIM this rush into the BIG BROKEN HIGHWAYS on the sides
		  Do this and he is stunned , fail and you get stunned and debuffed(-Attack Speed)
		  
	-In P3 he has a whole new set of moves , but just eat team revives until you win
   
	-Gear line priority : 
		-Weapon : PENETRATION | BOSS DMG | CRIT DAMAGE
		-Armor : COOLDOWN REDUCTION | CRIT RATE | CRIT DAMAGE
		-Accesories:
			=Rings : CRIT RATE | CRIT DAMAGE | ACCURACY
			=Pendant/Earrings : ATTACK SPEED | COOLDOWN REDUCTION
